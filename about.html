About Us

Sidmosh Global Limited popularly reffered to as DABIS FARM is a wholly indigeneous Nigeria Company in business of commercial poutry production , aquaculture and cattle farming. Established in 2018, the comapny is also involved in outside/extension services to educate farmers(practising and prospective) on the most viable husbandary practices

MISSION

To grow into a sustainable agricultural and food products provider and partner, provide high quality of farming and production operations, affordability of logistical distribution and protection of the environment and natural resources.
